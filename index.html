About Us

Spreading Kindness & Humanity

M.R Welfare Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to bringing positive change to society. We believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to live with dignity, hope, and basic human rights.

Through the dedication of our volunteers, donors, and partners, we continuously work to improve the lives of disadvantaged people and build a compassionate community.

Our Work

How We Help

Education

Providing books, school fees, and essential support to underprivileged children.

Medical Help

Organizing free medical camps, supplying medicines, and offering healthcare.

Food Distribution

Distributing nutritious food to the hungry and providing special Ramadan meals.

Disaster Relief

Emergency response and relief materials during floods, storms, and natural disasters.
